Welcome to Acme. This guide gets you from sign-up to your first widget in a few minutes.

## Create your account

1. Go to the Acme sign-up page and enter your work email.
2. Check your inbox for a verification link and click it.
3. Choose a workspace name — this is where your team and widgets live.
4. Invite teammates now, or skip and add them later from **Settings → Members**.

## Sign in

Open the Acme dashboard and sign in with your email and password. If single sign-on (SSO)
is enabled for your workspace, click **Continue with SSO** instead.

## Find your way around

- **Dashboard** — an overview of your widgets and recent activity.
- **Widgets** — create, configure, and publish widgets.
- **Settings** — manage members, billing, and security.
