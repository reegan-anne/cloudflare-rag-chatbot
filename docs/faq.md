## How do I reset my password?

1. On the sign-in page, click **Forgot password?**.
2. Enter the email address on your account and submit.
3. Open the reset email and click the link — it's valid for 60 minutes.
4. Choose a new password. You'll be signed in automatically.

If the email doesn't arrive, check your spam folder, then confirm you're using the same
address you signed up with.

## What plans are available?

Acme offers three plans:

- **Free** — one workspace, up to 3 widgets, community support.
- **Pro** — unlimited widgets, custom branding, and email support.
- **Enterprise** — SSO, audit logs, a dedicated success manager, and a custom SLA.

You can compare plans and upgrade any time from **Settings → Billing**.

## How do I change my plan?

Go to **Settings → Billing → Change plan**. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades
apply at the end of your current billing period.

## How do I contact support?

Pro and Enterprise customers can email support from **Settings → Help**. Everyone can ask
the chat assistant in the corner of this site — it answers from these docs.
